Combat

Move into enemies to attack. Weapon speed matters: fast weapons grant a bonus action, slow weapons cost a turn to recover. Reach weapons (spear, halberd) can lunge 2 tiles with P. Cleaving weapons hit a second target on kill.

Classes

Fighting-Man: Best combat, ranged mastery, companion formation bonus.
Cleric: Heals, turns undead, divine spells, gains power from god worship.
Magic-User: Powerful spells, fragile, intelligence boosts spell power.
Thief: Stealth, backstab unaware foes, trap mastery, smoke bombs.
Druid: Shapeshifts into wolf/bear/hawk, nature magic, walks through water.
Ranger: Ranged mastery, starts with wolf companion, tracks prey.
Warlock: Pact-bound to a god. Eldritch powers scale with piety. Apostasy kills.

Goal

Descend 16 floors, claim the Rune of Baal, then ascend back to the surface to escape. The dungeon grows more dangerous during your ascent.

Dungeon Branches

Portals on floors 5, 8, and 11 lead to underworld branches — Duat, Mictlan, and Tartarus. Each has unique monsters and a powerful boss guarding a rune artifact. Runes grant permanent game-breaking abilities. Completing all three is optional but dramatically increases your score.

Ranged & Thrown

F fires equipped ranged weapons (bows, crossbows, slings) or zaps equipped wands. V throws items (knives, javelins, axes). Wands are unidentified until first use.

Hunger

You'll starve if you don't eat. Press E to eat food from inventory. Food is auto-picked up when walking over it.
